Help & Support
How do I book a ticket?
›
Select your origin and destination from the home screen, choose your travel date, tap "Find Buses", pick a schedule, then select your seat and complete payment.
How does the wallet work?
›
Your GoRoute wallet is preloaded with ₱100,000 for testing. When you book a ticket, the fare is automatically deducted from your balance. Top-up via payment gateway coming soon.
Can I cancel my booking?
›
Cancellation and refund policies depend on the bus operator. Please contact support at least 24 hours before your departure for assistance.
