Here are the most common booking questions for bus, taxi, and motorcycle trips around Cebu.

How do I book?

Choose your origin, destination, travel date, and transport type. Then pick a schedule and confirm your booking.

Motorcycle coverage

Motorcycles are best for Cebu City, Mandaue City, Lapu-Lapu City, and nearby towns like Talisay, Consolacion, and Liloan. Farther trips may be unavailable or more expensive.

Can I book bus, taxi, and motorcycle trips?

Yes. The project supports bus, taxi, and motorcycle routes so you can travel around Cebu in one app.

Where are my tickets?

Open My Tickets from the dashboard to see your confirmed bookings and travel details.

What if no trips appear?

Try a different date or route pair. Some routes only have sample schedules on specific days.

Contact Support email: support@transport-booking-system.local
Simple rule: if it is within Metro Cebu, motorcycle service is usually available. Outside Metro Cebu, availability is not guaranteed.
